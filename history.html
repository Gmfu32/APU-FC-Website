OUR HISTORY

With over two decades of football played at a prime Kuala lumbur location so fundamental to the birth of APU FC in 1994, there is a great story to tell, full of ambition, star players, dramatic quests for trophy success and fashionable style.

There have been upsets and difficult challenges along the way as well that only add to the intrigue, and by visiting The Story of APU below, each twist and turn is recalled chapter by chapter.

With the rise of APU FC, the team quickly established itself as a formidable force in university football. Known for their tactical brilliance and unyielding spirit, APU FC has become a symbol of excellence both on and off the pitch. The club's journey from its inception to dominating inter-university tournaments is a story of determination, teamwork, and passion.


The Story of APU FC

Across nine chapters, the story of the Lions is revealed in words, images and videos - from the dreams of the founders realised in the best stadium in the land, to drawing big crowds with big stars, to modernising the club and winning trophies before a financial collapse and memorable recovery, and then the upward path to the current era.
