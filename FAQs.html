FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)



The APU Football Club is a student organization at Asia Pacific University that promotes football activities and events for students.

Any student enrolled at Asia Pacific University can join the APU Football Club.

You can become a member by registering online through our website or by attending one of our club meetings.

The club meets on Tuesdays or Thursdays at 5:15 PM at the university football field.

No, membership to the APU Football Club is completely free for all students.
