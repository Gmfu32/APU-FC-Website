ABOUT US







The Asia Pacific University Football Club is a dynamic and inclusive community of football enthusiasts, committed to promoting sportsmanship and teamwork within the university. Established in 2010, the club has grown to become one of the most recognized student organizations on campus, offering a platform for both competitive and recreational football activities. Our members range from seasoned players to beginners, all sharing a passion for the beautiful game.


We believe in the power of football to bring people together and foster a sense of belonging among students. Our club regularly organizes matches, tournaments, and training sessions, providing opportunities for skill development and social interaction. Whether you're looking to compete at a high level or simply enjoy the game with friends, APU Football Club has something for everyone.
